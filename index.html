Welcome to KaiZen. KaiZen uses a set of questions in a simple quiz app format, it uses questions from various mental illness screenings to provide information, resources and tools to help you understand and improve your mental health. Online screening tools are meant to be a quick snapshot of your mental health. If your results indicate you may be experiencing symptoms of a mental illness, consider sharing your results with someone.
